1. For Parents

Encourage your child/children to have an orginzed schedule.

Remind them to stay on top of homework and class work.

Symptoms to look out for
  • Dark Circles under eyes
  • Lack of concentration
  • Not active in hobbies or sports (if this relates to your childs daily life)
  • Constantly taking naps before and after school
  • Staying up longer than usuaul
Things to do
  • Be aware of how much sleep your child gets
  • Scheduling a sleep schedule if needed
  • Reasonable limits for them

2. For People that are sleep deprived

Encourage your children to have an orginzed schedule.

Remind them to stay on top of homework and class work.

Symptoms to look out for
  • Dizziness
  • Missing the ability to focus
  • Change in attitude
Things to do
  • Refraining from caffeine past noon
  • Go to bed on a consecutive time (the same times)
  • Sticking to the “bed time” even on some holidays off
  • Avoiding heavy meals 2-3 hours before sleeping
